State Spaces is coworking space and venue provider in Metro City. They offer physical spaces for study areas, meeting rooms, office spaces, conference halls, and workstations, including amenities for multimedia needs, and other needs.

System Overview

The following section provides an overview of the group’s implemented system based on State Spaces’ business processes. (Prototype of ONE function. Postgres and Django?)

Buildings

States Spaces maintains and provides venues for customers to reserve. Buildings owned by the company hold one or more venues. Each building is assigned a Building_ID along with a name, address, and city.

Agents & Teams

An agent manages one or more venues within the same building. Agents also manage teams that help with different aspects of venues. Agents have an Agent_ID and name, while teams have a Team_ID and job description.

Venues & Renovations

Each venue is assigned a Venue_ID along with a name, capacity, type, floor, floor_area in sqm, and an under_renovation boolean attribute reflective of the venue’s status. Renovations for each venue are kept track of, and each renovation has a Renovation_ID, start_date, and end_date.

Amenities

An amenity represents an item or furnishing of a particular make and model. It is characterized by its Amenity_ID, type, and description. Amenities are used by venues, and the quantity of a certain amenity for a particular venue is stored as a relationship attribute.

Customers & Reservations

Customers are characterized by their Customer_ID, name, birthday, and location. They can make reservations which, as an associative entity, have a Reservation_ID, participant quantity, and start and end times in both days and hours.

Reservation Rules

Customers can reserve one or more venues under the following conditions:

  • Participant quantity does not exceed the venue capacity
  • No overlapping time periods
  • Venue is not under renovation

Pencil bookings are not permitted, and cancelled or rescheduled reservations are reflected in the system.

Agent Responsibilities

It is assumed that customers deal with the agent corresponding to their targeted venue, and this agent enforces restrictions and handles the necessary arrangements for customer reservations (quotation, reservation confirmation, etc.).
